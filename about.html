Our purpose

Black Business in Reading is designed to showcase and signpost small and medium sized independent business outlets for potential customers and clients who require produce, products, and services of black and other ethnic minority cultures. It is hoped that this will connect, inspire, and grow these markets for all communities in Reading and beyond.


We hope you like this site and find our information interesting and helpful. You can contact us with any questions and feedback through our contact page.
