Black British commerce in Reading: a history

The history of black and other ethnic minority communities in Reading, a town in the UK which was once a regional hub for business and trade, stretches as far back as the Roman Empire. The story begins more than 2,000 years ago when the Romans conquered Britain and follows through to the British Empire during the slave trade era, and then through the Christian missionaries and continues even today.
